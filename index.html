About Us

Trusted & Faster Logistic Service Provider

JAPA is a logistics service company. The name JAPA is a local Nigerian slang which means 'run' or 'travel'. At JAPA delivery services, our aim is to ensure swift and safe delivery of items to our clients' desired destinations.

With a dedicated team and years of experience, we offer reliable courier solutions for individuals and businesses. Our services include same-day delivery, express shipping, and real-time tracking to keep you updated every step of the way.

We pride ourselves on professionalism, transparency, and customer satisfaction. Whether you are sending a single package or managing bulk shipments, JAPA is committed to making your delivery experience seamless and stress-free.
